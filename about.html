Our Story

Sweet Food Stories is a website created to celebrate the joy of cooking and the stories behind every recipe. Every dish has a memory, a moment, or a person connected to it, and this project brings those stories to life through simple, warm, and inviting design.

The purpose of this website is to share recipes, personal food memories, and cultural traditions in a way that feels friendly and meaningful. Whether it’s a dessert passed down through generations or a new recipe discovered by accident, each story adds flavor to the experience.

This project was created as part of a web design course, and it reflects my interest in combining storytelling with food culture. The goal is to make visitors feel inspired, connected, and hungry for more.
